<br/>
A Power Booster is a rare item obtained from defeating bosses.  Prehardmode bosses don't drop power boosters unless the config option is enabled.  It adds 10 levels to an item (These levels do not count towards the level 40 cap or give critical strike chance.).  They can only be used once per item.  To apply a power booster to an item, place the item into the enchanting table then click the power booster onto the item in the table as if you were merging two stacks of the same item.  You can also shift left click the power booster from your inventory.  Power boosters drop from boss bags in Expert/Master mode at the same rates from the table.<br/>
