=== Enchantment Effects ===
Enchantments allow customization of your items with various effects.  Some are very basic stat upgrades such as [[Damage Enchantment|Damage]], [[Critical Strike Chance Enchantment|Critical Strike Chance]], [[Defense Enchantment|Defense]], [[Life Steal Enchantment|Life Steal]].  Other are more unique such as: Hitting all enemies in an area ([[One For All Enchantment|One For All]]), Dealing massive damage but having a long recovery ([[All For One Enchantment|All For One]]), Flames that spread between enemies ([[World Ablaze Enchantment|World Ablaze]]), Max health true damage ([[God Slayer Enchantment|God Slayer]]).<br/>
=== Capacity Cost ===
Each enchantment has a capacity cost. This cost is subtracted from the item enchantment capacity.<br/>

=== Utility Enchantments ===
[[Utility Enchantments]] are non-damage based enchantments.  They can be placed into any of the enchantment slots, but only utility enchantments can be placed into the utility slot.<br/>
