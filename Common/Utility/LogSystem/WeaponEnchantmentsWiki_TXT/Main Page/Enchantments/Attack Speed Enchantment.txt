[[Enchantments]]<br/>
The '''Attack Speed Enchantments''' are [[enchantments]] that decrease an item's [https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Use_time use time], resulting in more uses per second. Additionally, it increases mining speed on tools, the fire rate of minion's that shoot projectiles and reduces how long it takes a fish to bite when used on fishing poles.  If the enchantment gives 10%(configurable) or more attack speed, it will also enable [https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Autoswing autoswing].<br/>

The Attack Speed Enchantment Basic can appear in the  [[Witch]]'s shop after the  [https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Eater_of_Worlds Eater of Worlds] or  [https://terraria.fandom.com/wiki/Brain_of_Cthulhu Brain of Cthulhu] have been defeated for <span>14</span>https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/terraria_gamepedia/images/1/1f/Gold_Coin.png/revision/latest?cb=20200516213029&format=original <span>70</span>https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/terraria_gamepedia/images/4/4e/Silver_Coin.png/revision/latest?cb=20200516221827&format=original.<br/>
